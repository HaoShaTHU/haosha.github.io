My research interests lie in ML and NLP, with a specific focus on the training and evaluation of LMs. I am also paying attention to the evaluation and methods of LLM agents. My goal is to make language models truly capable of solving problems in both the physical and digital worlds.

Currently, I am a research intern at Language Technologies Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Chenyan Xiong. During my exchange at the University of Hong Kong, I am fortunate to work with Prof. Tao Yu. Previously, I collaborated with Prof. Xuming Hu and Prof. Lijie Wen at School of Software, Tsinghua University.
